What is PlexPy?

PlexPy is a python based web application for monitoring, analytics, and notifications for your Plex Media Server.


PlexPy (pronounced "Plex Pie") is a 3rd party application that you can run along side your Plex Media Server to monitor activity and track various statistics. Most importantly, these statistics include what has been watched, who watched it, when and where they watched it, and how it was watched. The only thing missing is "why they watched it", but who am I to question your 42 plays of Frozen. All statistics are presented in a nice and clean interface with many tables and graphs, which makes it easy to brag about your server to everyone else.
